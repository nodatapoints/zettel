\[ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{f(ax)}{x} = \lim_{y \rightarrow \infty} \frac{f(y)}{\frac{1}{a}y} = a \lim_{y \rightarrow \infty} \frac{f(y)}{y} = ac \]
